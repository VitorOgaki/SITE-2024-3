PORSCHE 911 Turbo 3.3 Coupé in PTS: Graphite Grey Metallic

Extraordinary conditions, this 930 Turbo is totally unrestored and original in every aspects. Original painting, original wheels, still present all the protective wax under the body and normally accident free.

The car was sold new form the Italian Porsche dealer near Modena at the first owner who has kept the car until 1990 to sell after to his friend. Extremely rare for a car like this the car remains always in the same family from 1990 to our days. Now the car has only 64.475 km documented with a complete Porsche history, as a metter of fact a lot of maintenances were done and stamped on service book from the Porsche Classic Center Padova Est Verardini, as the last inspection on January 2024.
